The 28th Annual Big Dance

Bloom in the Dark

A mystical night in the enchanted garden.

The Big Dance returns on Friday, May 15, 2026 for a twilight celebration of music, dancing, and after-hours wonder. Expect candlelit florals, midnight waltzes, and a ballroom that feels like it opened somewhere between a dream and a secret garden.

The Invitation

Wander through a botanical wonderland where chandeliers shimmer like constellations and every hall echoes with a different rhythm. For nearly three decades, the Big Dance has transformed our campus into a realm of mystery and magic. Join the 28th annual celebration of dance, discovery, and the spirit of the garden.

Dress the Part

Celebrate the 28th Big Dance. You may arrive:

  • Botanical: Adorned in florals, greenery, or botanical accents.
  • Whimsical: As a creature who dwells within the garden.
  • Yourself: Festive attire is always welcome.

A costume contest will celebrate the most creative transformations.

Good to Know

  • Footwear: Soft-soled dance shoes or socks only. No street shoes are allowed inside Roble Gym.
  • Volunteers: Stanford student volunteers receive free admission.
  • Questions: Email the committee for ticketing, volunteering, or accessibility support.

The Big Dance Legacy

This night is brand new, but it belongs to a long tradition. The archive carries the history forward, from early years in the nineties through decades of dancing, dressing up, and staying far too late with friends.

Browse the photo archive for a glimpse of past Big Dance eras, then imagine this year's enchanted garden joining that story.
